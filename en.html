Dermatology

Welcome to Dr. Judit Krizsa's private dermatology clinic!

Our goal is to provide dermatological care with the most modern methods and tools and at the highest professional level.

Our clinic is located in Szeged, at Szent István tér 13, in the heart of the city center, easily accessible by public transport and by car, access is barrier-free.

Scheduling an appointment is simple: patients line up at the reception desk, by phone or after registering online, so that they can receive the necessary care as quickly and smoothly as possible.

During our work, the needs and demands of the patient are always at the center. In addition to medical examinations, we also provide cosmetic treatments and counseling if needed.

We hope that you will have a pleasant experience with us and that we can welcome you among our satisfied patients.
If you have any further questions, please contact us.
